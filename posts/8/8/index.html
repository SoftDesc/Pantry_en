General:

How to enter receipts:

“Print empty QR codes then enter data” - a case where QR codes containing only unique GUIDs are printed and affixed. Then, when scanning the QR codes, the item parameters are specified: recipe, volume, and quantity.

“Enter data then print QR codes” - Data for recipes, quantities, and volumes is entered, then QR codes containing the entered information are printed and affixed.

Reminders:

If the “Remind about expiration date” flag is set, the program will display a notification upon startup for items that are nearing their expiration date. The “Number of storage months before reminder” parameter sets the period when notifications for a product begin to appear.

Default:

Default shelf life, months - the shelf life that is automatically set when creating new items.

Supplement help:

If this option is enabled, it becomes possible to edit the help text and save it in md-format.
